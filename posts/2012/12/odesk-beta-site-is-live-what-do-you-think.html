oDesk beta site is live! What do you think?

December 4 2012, 7:53 PM  by Mar Cejas

oDeskers are getting a first peek at the new beta site.

The updated design will help you read, navigate, and take care of business easier than ever. But it’s more than just a pretty face! Under the surface there’s a major code overhaul, making the site quicker and more consistent. Even better, oDesk can roll out new functionality and fix problems fast.

New capabilities are coming your way faster than ever before, including:

  • A new My Contractors page that lets you track your current team, as well as past hires and saved contractors
  • Message Center updates with upcoming fixes to address issues you've shared with us
  • More powerful search and Job Feeds to help you find the jobs and contractors you want

To access the new site:

Log into oDesk and look for the “Try the New Site!” link at the top of every page or if you are not a beta tester try putting this in to your browser address https://www.odesk.com/beta/opt-in. Give it a try! if you want to go back to the old site just put this to your address bar https://www.odesk.com/beta/opt-out and go!
